# Bebouz Shooter

**Bebouz Shooter** is a dark top-down survival shooter made for the **Aristos Interactive Jam**.

Plunged into a pitch-black arena, you must survive as long as possible against endless hordes of infected creatures. Your only lifeline is your flashlight… and your weapon.

---

## 🧟 Gameplay

### 🔦 Dynamic Lighting
The map is completely dark. You must rely on:
- your character’s **flashlight cone**
- the **glow of your bullets**
to spot enemies hiding in the shadows.

### 👾 Enemy Types
Fight against **3 distinct types of enemies**:
- **Zombies**: relentless, slow-moving horde  
- **Ghosts**: unpredictable enemies moving in a zigzag / sine-wave pattern  
- **Dashers**: wait in the dark before lunging at you with incredible speed  

### ⬆️ Upgrades
Collect **golden and glowing cubes** to:
- **permanently increase** your fire rate, or
- trigger a **temporary 5-second multi-shot spread** to clear the swarm

### 🩸 Juicy Feedback
Defeated enemies leave **permanent blood splatters** on the ground, with dynamic SFX for a satisfying arcade feel.

---

## 🎮 Controls

- **W, A, S, D** or **Arrow Keys**: Move  
- **Mouse**: Aim flashlight + weapon  
- **Left Click**: Shoot
